In a Nutshell

How do you squish 30 years of life into an elevator pitch? Let’s narrow it down to simply who I am today. Today I’m someone who is passionate about sustainable development and equality. I work towards these goals daily by taking part in global efforts to make education inclusive and accessible to all. My niche spot in the bigger picture lies behind the scenes, working on tools and processes that can help us all get there in a better, faster, and more resilient way.

Career Highlights

Over the past five years, I’ve experienced flow while writing and editing content used for digital learning, documentation of products and processes, marketing, and professional training. I’ve also enjoyed working with project management, product onboarding - both on-site and online, and the optimization of internal processes. These experiences have taught me the importance of good time and self-management, decision-making, teamwork, and design thinking. Knowing that I'm contributing to the bigger picture, one task at a time, is my rocket fuel. And sharing in success with my peers and colleagues gives me life!

Web Development & Data

Web is where it’s at! 59% of the world’s population was online in 2020. I’m currently working on HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, and MySQL through personal projects. I also plan on exploring Python, MongoDB, and APIs soon. One thing I absolutely love about coding is using it to pick needles out of haystacks! It’s been estimated that there was more than 5,000 GB of data per person on Earth in 2020 - a sizable portion of that being personal data. That’s one big ass haystack! While some may focus on how they could use this data to sell products, or give a particular politician an electoral advantage, I want to focus on how we can use data to help people better their lives on their own terms.

Optimization & Automation

It’s time to level up! When you are part of a team that consists of the best and the brightest, the last thing they should be doing is manually updating excel sheets. It’s my belief that we really harness our individual human potential whenever we engage in meaningful work. That’s why I’m currently learning how to build automation scripts with the help of APIs - for myself, and for others!

EdTech to TechEd

This far, I have mostly worked as a content editor for companies that focus on using technology to help deliver education. Now I’m ready to take that effort to the next level: helping to further develop technology to make education more inclusive and globally available. World Economic Forum recently listed the most sought-after skills to have by 2025. It's my opinion that all of these skills can be built through programming! That’s why it’s one of my personal goals to help make technology more approachable through education - encouraging everyone, no matter what their background might be, to explore all the possibilities that learning programming can unlock.
